About PeerBridge

A student-driven peer learning platform designed to connect learners and mentors within the same academic community.

The platform helps students discover peers who excel in specific subjects, schedule learning sessions, exchange knowledge, and build meaningful academic connections.

Learning becomes easier when students support students.

By combining subject-based matching, flexible scheduling, peer reviews, and community-driven collaboration, PeerBridge creates an environment where students can learn together, grow together, and succeed together.

Whether a student needs help understanding difficult concepts or wants to share knowledge with others, PeerBridge provides a structured and trusted space for academic collaboration.
